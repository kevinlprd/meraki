Current Status and Issues

Currently Juniper 12 Port non-PoE switch for AV
Switch is managed by Network
  • Slow - AV team takes long time to make any changes or test any new AV devices due to lack of control
  • Incapable - When swithes have any failures, AV team cannot restore the service
  • Cost - AV/Network team have to travel to the regional offices to turn on the service

What we need for new Teams Room System

  • 48 ports PoE Switch to support new MS Teams room system
  • AV team easy to manage and configure the switch
  • Zero touch provisioning to support plug-n-play to reduce travel
  • AV team easy to replace the defective switch and restore the service
